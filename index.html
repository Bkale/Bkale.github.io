About Me

DIGITAL CREATIVE

FRONT_END DEVELOPER

I'm an experienced front-end developer with proficient communication skills and a broad range of leadership experiences.

Highly creative and curious, I'm always looking to pick up a new programming language. I am currently a teaching assistant at New York Code & Design Academy.

Born and raised in Lagos Nigeria. My family and I migrated to the US in 2003 where I continued my studies at St.Benedict's Preparatory School.

I graduated from William Paterson University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 2014.

Upon graduating I worked as a SQL Developer/ Software Developer at Medassets and followed that experience by joining the Web immersive program at General Assembly in New York, NY. Upon completing the program, I got a position at New York Code & Design Academy where I help new developers learn and master various programming languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, React, Redux among others.

I enjoy teaching because it helps me understand code at a fundamental level and I get ask tons of unique questions about a concept that forces me to understand why programs work the way they do.

When I'm not working (whatever the hell that means), I'm writing or building a website for someone. I love lending a hand, so hit me up (bonus points if it has to do with photography:)).
